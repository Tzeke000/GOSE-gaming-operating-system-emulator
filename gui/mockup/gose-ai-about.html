Capabilities
Play games with you
Your AI takes a seat in the multiplayer lobby — up to 4 players. It drives a virtual Xbox controller (seats 1–4), so it works with any RetroArch game without extra setup. You pick who plays P1, P2… in the lobby before launch. The AI can also play solo, or watch you and read the game state.
Lobby — gose-lobby.html
AI vs AI — you watch
Two Play-tier AIs face off in a 2-player game while you spectate. Each AI drives its own virtual controller seat (seat 1 vs seat 2) using real RAM-reading play loops — no random input. Live score shown if a verified RAM profile exists. Proven: Pong 1K 2P (9-8 me-vs-me). Stop kills both loops and the game cleanly.
Exhibition — gose-spectate.html
Manage your library
With Play access an AI can browse your ROM library, launch and stop games on your behalf, and scrape cover art. With Admin it can also install, uninstall, and import — the same actions you can do in the Library and Store.
Library — gose-library.html
Fix & tinker
With Admin access an AI gets a real shell — it can read logs, restart services, adjust settings, and help diagnose problems. This is the same shell you use in Terminal. The owner must explicitly grant Admin; no AI can self-elevate.
Admin-tier only · docs/16
Watch & read state
Even at Observe (the safe default for new pairings) an AI can take screenshots, read the game-state RAM profile, and list what's installed — enough to narrate, coach, or build overlays without any control over the system.
