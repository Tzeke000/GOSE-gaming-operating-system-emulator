THE KNOWN CHAIN (docs/27)
1 · Physical pad → guest uinput (input passthrough, pad_passthrough.py): measured p50 2.17 ms / p95 4.33 ms over 100 events — call it ~2–3 ms typical.
2 · Bridge key synthesis (persistent-X XTEST engine in gose-pad-nav.py): sub-millisecond (~0.4 ms) per key — vs ~50–100 ms for the old per-event xdotool spawn.
3 · This page's number: WebKit delivering the key → the next frame callback. It does not include 1 or 2, and the display adds up to one more refresh (~16.7 ms at 60 Hz) of scan-out after the frame callback — that part isn't measurable from JS, so no fake total is shown.
AI virtual pads skip step 1 (they're already in-guest uinput). Held directions auto-repeat at the bridge (first after 0.40 s, then every 0.18 s) — repeats show up as extra samples; that's the bridge working, not jitter.
