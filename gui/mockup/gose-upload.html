Add a Game

Send a ROM (or a BIOS file) to GOSE straight from this device — your PC or phone. It lands in the right folder and shows up in your Library. On the VM, open this page from the host at http://127.0.0.1:8780/gose-upload.html; on a real device, browse to its IP.
Provide your own legally-obtained game files — GOSE never ships ROMs or BIOS. Large discs (PS2/GameCube) can take a while; keep this tab open until it finishes.
